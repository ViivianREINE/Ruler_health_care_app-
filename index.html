Emergency Help for Rural India

Rural Emergency Assist is a lifesaving mobile app designed specifically for villages in India, providing quick support during medical or safety emergencies even with low network connectivity.

Download Now

Our Mission

To empower rural communities with immediate access to emergency assistance, medical guidance, and local support networks through innovative technology that works even with limited connectivity.

Works Offline

Critical features available without internet connection

First Aid Guides

Voice-based instructions for common emergencies

One-Tap SOS

Send location via SMS or Bluetooth when internet is weak

App Features

Offline Voice Guides

Step-by-step first aid instructions for snake bites, burns, heart attacks, fractures, and more in Hindi and local languages.

Bluetooth SOS

Send emergency alerts via Bluetooth mesh network when cellular signal is weak.

Connect to Helpers

Directly contact nearby village volunteers, ASHA workers, and the closest healthcare centers.

Offline Maps

Access maps and hospital lists without internet connection.

AI Symptom Checker

Analyze symptoms and provide step-by-step guidance until help arrives.

Voice Assistant

Friendly female voice interface in Hindi and local languages for hands-free operation.

How It Works

1

Download & Setup

Install the app and complete the simple setup process. The app works immediately, even offline.

2

Emergency Situation

In case of emergency, open the app or use the voice command to activate assistance.

3

Get Immediate Help

Use one-tap SOS, access first-aid guides, or connect with nearby volunteers and healthcare providers.

4

Follow Guidance

The AI assistant provides step-by-step instructions while help is on the way.

Connect with Helpers

Village Volunteers

Trained community members ready to assist during emergencies.

How to connect: Use the app to find nearby volunteers and send assistance requests.

ASHA Workers

Accredited Social Health Activists providing basic healthcare support.

How to connect: Direct messaging and location-based matching in the app.

Ambulance Services

Quick access to the nearest available ambulance with estimated arrival time.

How to connect: One-tap ambulance request with automatic location sharing.

AI Symptom Checker

Describe your symptoms and get AI-powered guidance on next steps.

Namaste! I'm your health assistant. Please describe your symptoms or emergency situation.

My father is having chest pain and difficulty breathing.

This could be a serious condition. Please have him sit down and rest immediately. I'm connecting you to nearby emergency services. Meanwhile, can you tell me more about his symptoms? Does the pain radiate to his arm or jaw?
